Self-balancing Two Wheeled Robot

August 2016

The basic purpose of our project was to demonstrate the techniques involved in balancing an unstable robotic platform on two wheels and to design a complete discrete digital control system that would provide the needed stability. Basically, our bot prevented itself from falling by giving acceleration to the wheels according to its inclination from the vertical. If the bot gets tilts by an angle, than in the frame of the wheels, the center of mass of the bot will experience a pseudo force which will apply a torque opposite to the direction of tilt.
